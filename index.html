Notifications are disruptive by nature. They keep you from what you're working on, or interrupt you in social settings.

Not all notifications are bad, like event reminders and messages from friends. It can be hard to weed out what notifications are genuinely useful.

Hush is a guide to help make sense of your phone's notifications. We've collected a list of apps that we've found notoriously intrusive, and have developed guides to help cut out the unnecessary bits.

Every app has a badge indicating how many issues we've found with it. Click on the app for a brief writeup describing how to keep the app quiet.

If you have any questions, feel free to say hi.
